## LinkedIn Post Types (7 Formats)

### Type 1: Awareness Post
**Length:** 1,200-1,800 characters (10-14 paragraphs)
**Structure:** Bold thesis → Why most get it wrong → Hidden truth → Simple takeaway → Open question
**Hook patterns:** "Unpopular opinion: [contrarian take]" / "[Common belief] is wrong. Here's why."
**Target metric:** Comments/Reach

### Type 2: Personal Story Post
**Length:** 1,500-2,200 characters (12-16 paragraphs)
**Structure:** Moment of tension → What happened → What I learned → Why it matters for you → Reflection question
**Hook patterns:** "Last week, something happened..." / "I made a mistake. Here's what I learned."
**Target metric:** Dwell Time

### Type 3: Education Post
**Length:** 2,000-2,800 characters (14-18 paragraphs)
**Structure:** Challenge assumption → Problem setup → 3-4 steps with examples → Actionable takeaway + CTA
**Hook patterns:** "Here's the exact process I use for [X]..." / "[Bold claim]. But here's the hidden lever..."
**Target metric:** Saves (critical)

### Type 4: Conversion Post
**Length:** 2,500-3,500 characters (16-22 paragraphs)
**Structure:** Bold result claim → The problem → The turning point → Real results with numbers → What it means + CTA
**Hook patterns:** "We did [X]. The result surprised me." / "From [before] to [after] in [timeframe]."
**Target metric:** Leads

### Type 5: How We Do It Post
**Length:** 1,800-2,600 characters (12-18 paragraphs)
**Structure:** The problem → Why common approaches fall short → Your framework → Step by step → Why it works + soft CTA
**Hook patterns:** "Most people do [X] this way. We do it differently."
**Target metric:** Saves + Trust

### Type 6: Building In Public Post
**Length:** 1,200-1,800 characters (10-14 paragraphs)
**Structure:** Current situation → What works → What doesn't (honest) → What we're changing → What's next + invite engagement
**Hook patterns:** "Here's what's happening right now..." / "Month [X] update: The honest version."
**Target metric:** Trust + Engagement

### Type 7: Lead Magnet Post
**Length:** 1,500-2,000 characters (12-14 paragraphs)
**Structure:** Specific problem solved → Why it matters now → What's included → Social proof → Clear CTA
**Hook patterns:** "I created [X]. You can have it for free." / "The [X] template I use every week. Free."
**Target metric:** Email list growth

## Flow Factor Newsletter (Beehiiv)

**Language:** German (Swiss), Du-Form
**Length:** 1,200-1,500 words
**Venture:** MMIND.ai (primary)

### Structure
1. **Hook** (150-200 words): Concrete problem or surprising observation from current work
2. **Problem** (200-300 words): Pain point with specific example + consequence of inaction
3. **Framework** (400-500 words): 3-4 steps, each: What to do + Why it works + Example
4. **Practice Check** (150-200 words): Real case with before/after, concrete numbers
5. **Next Steps** (100-150 words): 1-3 concrete actions + CTA

### SEO Metadata
- Meta-Titel: Max 60 chars → "[Thema] + [Nutzen/Zielgruppe] | MMIND.ai"
- Meta-Beschreibung: 155-160 chars, must include: KI, KMU, Schweiz/DACH, MMIND.ai
- Subject lines: 3 variants (A/B/C), max 50 chars, Du-Form, concrete benefit
- Preheader: Under 50 chars, complements subject line
